Eric Burr

After high school I went to Jiangxi China to teach english to eight year olds. While there we were able to go on several vacations. On one of the trips I got a fever, and growing up whenever I got a fever I would hallucinate about being chased throughout out the night. I spent a day in one of the world's largest cities hallucinating by one of the other teachers! (I haven't done this in 10+ years). My whole life I have wanted to go do things that I don't know how to do. I just like to do things, such as living in China. My hobbies include snow skiing, mountain biking, reading, and playing sports!
What makes you nervous?
The things that make me most nervous in life are as follows: great white sharks, angry wife, getting locked in a freezer, forgetting to take out the trash two weeks in a row.
What has been your greatest moment as a developer?
There are a few ways to define "greatest", but the first thing that comes to my mind is most "enjoyable". I remember working on one of my first apps, and after spending about two weeks working on it, stepping back planning what I wanted it to look like, and discussing the different ways to get there. The conversation was about authentication, using multiple collections in my database, and the transfer of data. It was the first time I thought, "man, this is pretty fun"
What is your favorite thing from pop culture?
I really like the Utah Jazz, but i'm not sure if that falls under pop culture. I really like music, and movies and i'm not really one to get tired of what is mainstream right now. I never got burnt out of the Marvel movies, and I can still enjoy the Backstreet Boys original album, or even what's new from Dan + Shay.
